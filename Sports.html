SPORTS

Double WKT Tournamnet

Rules & Regulations:

1. The match will be played on knock out basis with leather ball.

2. Each innings consist of two overs. Bowler will bowl only one over.

3. No ball and wide ball will be paying a licenced according to ICC cricket rules. There will be free hit for no ball, if any.

4. Teams can choose maximum 03 players squad. Any player apart from the squad cannot interfere.

5. Team should reach 30 mins before commencement of the match after which a walkover will be awarded.

6. In all events, the decision of thr jury of thr umpires will be final.

7. If any match ends in a draw/tie the winner will be awarded or judged by super ball. If again the scores are equal then the spin of coin option will be adopted.

8. If batsman is out, 2 runs will be deducted from the total score.

9. The bowler is allowed to take only 5 meters runup, not more then that.

10. No LBW and other rules, applicable according to ICC rules,

11. 9 players will be provided by the host school.

3A Side Volleyball

Rules & Regulations:

1. A team should consist of three players.

2. Three players per team on court [2 boys + 1 girl (compulsory)].

3. Game of 11 points/best of 3.

4. Fixture will be drawn on the spot.

Mixed Relay

Rules & Regulations:

1. 4X200 relay.

2. A team should consist of 4 players(2 boys + 2 girls).

3. Mandatory - Shoes to be worn. Atheletes can wear spike shoes.

4. Teams will be selected for finals on time trial basis.
